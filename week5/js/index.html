ABOUT

“We=Link: Ten Easy Pieces” is a special online exhibition sponsored by Chronus Art Center (CAC). It shows the CAC’s response to the coronavirus pandemic and widespread lock-down. The title “We=Link: Ten Easy Pieces” implies the American actor Jack Nicholson’s iconic movie Five Easy Pieces with a subtle twist on WeChat , the popular Chinese social media platform.

Ten Pieces

The title “We=Link: Ten Easy Pieces” denotes a community of solidarity as a network of empowerment. The ten Easy Pieces, featuring new commissions by the artists aaajiao, Tega Brain & Sam Lavigne, JODI, LI Weiyi, Slime Engine and YE Funa in conjunction with works by Evan Roth, Helmut Smits, Yangachi and Raphaël Bastide.

n22.230210e113.940187.hk | Evan Roth

n22.230210e113.940187.hk is a network located video depicting a landscape in Hong Kong where internet submarine fiber optic cables meet the land. The artists, Evan Roth, tends to depict a physical connection between the viewer and the landscape.

evasive.tech | Raphaël Bastide

evasive.tech is quarantine-driven coding performances created by Raphaël Bastide, starting March 17 2020. evasive.tech is a series of episodes, each being envisioned and coded on a daily basis.

Screen time | Helmut Smitz

Screen Time is a community clock connecting strangers through their mobile backgrounds. Participants are invited to take part by making a screenshot of their mobile device lock screen and submitting it to the allocated time slot.
